About me

My name is Stacey Smith. I am originally from Zimbabwe where I taught French for nine years. I then taught French for two years at the American International School in Egypt before relocating to the United States with my husband who is a Colorado native. While my native language is English, I speak several languages fluently including French, Spanish, and Shona. My background in linguistics and education has enabled me to transfer my linguistic skills from the knowledge of human languages to mastering computer languages.

I am a highly motivated, organized, hardworking, multi-lingual person with excellent analytical and problem-solving skills. I am a great team player and skilled at working in a multi-cultural environment and collaborating on small and large teams.
